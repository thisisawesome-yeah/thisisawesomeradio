About THISISAWESOMERADIO

We are an independent web radio from Bremen with a simple mission: music that deserves to be heard. No format restrictions, no playlist optimization for algorithms, just small commercial breaks (to cover licensing costs).

Our small team writes reviews, provides context and broadcasts seven days a week - from jazz to rock, from singer-songwriter to industrial. Because good music doesn't need labels.
